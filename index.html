Teryaki Chicken

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Secret Sauce 1/3 Cup
Chicken Thigh 2 Packages

Step 1: Cook chicken in a pan on the stove at medium.

Step 2:Turn stove to low and add sauce

Step 3: Cook on low for 2 minutes or until sauce thickens

Step 4: EAT!!!!

Raviolli's

  • Time: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato Sauce 1 Can
Raviollis 2 Packages

Step 1: Fill A Pan With Water

Step 2:Boil Water On Stove On High

Step 3: Add Raviollis

Step 4: Cook for 5 minutes or until Raviollis float to the top
